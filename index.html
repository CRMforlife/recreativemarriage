About the Book

ReCreative Marriage is a practical guide for couples who want to deepen their relationship through creative methods.

  • Learn to use creative methods to improve communication
  • Discover new ways to express love and appreciation
  • Develop new rituals and traditions together
  • Deepen emotional connection through art and creativity
  • Build a stronger foundation for your shared future

Who Is This Book For?

This book is perfect for couples who want to:

  • Deepen their relationship in new ways
  • Find creative solutions to daily challenges
  • Improve their communication
  • Build a stronger connection

About the Author

Jania Aaltonen

Jania is a relationship therapist and creative methods expert with over 15 years of experience helping couples. She developed the ReCreative Marriage method by combining traditional couples therapy tools with creative exercises.
